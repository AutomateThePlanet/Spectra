# Register citizen with in-person verification and resume wizard after 12 hours

## Preconditions

None

## Steps

1. Navigate to the citizen registration portal
2. Complete Step 1 (Personal Information) with valid data
3. Complete Step 2 (Contact Information) with email and phone verification
4. Note that verified email/phone tokens are preserved
5. Close the browser without completing registration
6. Wait 12 hours
7. Return to the registration portal in a new browser session
8. Verify wizard resumes from Step 3 (last completed step was Step 2)
9. Verify email and phone verification statuses are still marked as verified (no re-verification required)
10. Complete Step 3 (Address)
11. Step 4: Select 'In-person Verification' as identity verification method
12. Complete Step 5 (Account Setup) and Step 6 (Confirmation)
13. Submit registration

## Expected Result

Wizard progress is restored from local storage after 12 hours (within 24-hour window). Verified email/phone tokens are preserved. Account is created in 'Pending Verification' status with limited access. Reminder emails are scheduled for 7, 14, and 21 days.
