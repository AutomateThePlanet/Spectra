# Register citizen with document upload identity verification

## Preconditions

User has valid Bulgarian ID card (лична карта) scans in JPEG/PNG format, minimum 300 DPI or 1000x600 pixels

## Steps

1. Complete Steps 1-3 of registration wizard with valid Bulgarian citizen data
2. Step 4: Select 'Document Upload' as identity verification method
3. Upload front side of Bulgarian ID card (JPEG, under 10 MB, meets resolution requirements)
4. Upload back side of Bulgarian ID card
5. Wait for OCR processing to extract document number, expiry date, and name
6. Review OCR results displayed on screen
7. Confirm OCR results are correct
8. Verify document is not expired (expiry date is in the future)
9. Click 'Next'
10. Complete Step 5 (Account Setup) with password and accept Terms and Conditions
11. Complete Step 6 (Confirmation) and submit registration

## Expected Result

OCR successfully extracts document details, document is validated as not expired, account is created, welcome email is sent, citizen is redirected to dashboard

## Test Data

ID card images: front.jpg (2MB, 1200x800px), back.jpg (2MB, 1200x800px), Document expiry: future date
