# Verify consent records retained for 5 years after withdrawal

## Preconditions

Citizen has withdrawn consent

## Steps

1. Withdraw consent for a specific consent type
2. Note timestamp of withdrawal
3. Verify consent record is retained in the system
4. Verify retention period is set to 5 years from withdrawal date
5. Confirm record includes withdrawal timestamp and original consent details

## Expected Result

Consent records are retained for 5 years after withdrawal, as proof of processing lawfulness during the consent period

## Test Data

Consent withdrawal timestamp
