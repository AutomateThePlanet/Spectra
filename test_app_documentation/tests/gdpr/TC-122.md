# Verify strictly necessary cookies cannot be disabled

## Preconditions

Cookie banner is displayed

## Steps

1. Display cookie banner
2. Click 'Customize' button
3. Attempt to toggle off 'Strictly Necessary Cookies'
4. Verify toggle is disabled or grayed out
5. Verify tooltip or message explains these cookies cannot be disabled

## Expected Result

Strictly Necessary Cookies are always active and cannot be disabled. Includes session cookies and CSRF tokens

## Test Data

Cookie banner in customize mode
