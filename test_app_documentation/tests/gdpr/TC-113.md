# Verify payment records retained with anonymized data

## Preconditions

Citizen has payment history

## Steps

1. Ensure citizen account has payment records
2. Note payment transaction IDs
3. Submit account deletion request
4. Wait for processing to complete
5. Access payment records (as administrator)
6. Verify payment records are retained
7. Verify citizen data in payment records is anonymized
8. Confirm retention period is 10 years (tax/accounting obligation)

## Expected Result

Payment records are retained with anonymized citizen data for 10 years (tax/accounting obligation)

## Test Data

Citizen account with completed payment transactions
