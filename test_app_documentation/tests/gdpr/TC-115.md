# Verify citizen receives confirmation email after deletion

## Preconditions

Citizen has provided alternative email address for deletion confirmation

## Steps

1. Provide alternative email address during deletion request
2. Submit account deletion request
3. Wait for processing to complete
4. Check alternative email inbox
5. Verify confirmation email is received
6. Verify email contains deletion confirmation details

## Expected Result

Citizen receives confirmation email to an alternative email address (since the account email is deleted)

## Test Data

Alternative email: alternative@example.com
