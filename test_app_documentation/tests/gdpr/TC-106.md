# Verify citizen can update email address with re-verification

## Preconditions

Citizen is logged into the portal

## Steps

1. Navigate to profile settings
2. Click to edit email address
3. Enter new email address
4. Submit the change
5. Check new email inbox for verification link
6. Click verification link
7. Verify email address is updated in profile

## Expected Result

Citizen can update email address through self-service. New email requires re-verification before the change is applied

## Test Data

Old email: citizen@example.com, New email: newemail@example.com
