# Verify marketing communications opt-out takes effect within 24 hours

## Preconditions

Citizen has opted in to marketing communications

## Steps

1. Navigate to notification preferences
2. Locate marketing communications toggle
3. Opt out of marketing communications
4. Note timestamp of opt-out
5. Wait 24 hours
6. Verify no marketing communications are sent after opt-out takes effect
7. Verify opt-out is recorded in consent records

## Expected Result

Marketing communications opt-out is instant and takes effect within 24 hours. No marketing messages are sent after the 24-hour window

## Test Data

Citizen account with marketing consent enabled
