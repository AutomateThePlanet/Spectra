# Account remains locked for 30 minutes after lockout

## Preconditions

User account is locked due to failed login attempts

## Steps

1. Wait for 29 minutes after account lockout
2. Attempt to login with correct credentials
3. Observe the response
4. Wait 1 additional minute (total 30 minutes)
5. Attempt to login with correct credentials again

## Expected Result

Login attempt at 29 minutes fails with lockout message. Login attempt at 30 minutes succeeds and user is authenticated. ACCOUNT_UNLOCKED event is logged when lockout expires.

## Test Data

Valid credentials for locked account
