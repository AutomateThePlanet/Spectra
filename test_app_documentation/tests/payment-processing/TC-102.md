# Fee waiver automatically applied for eligible citizen

## Preconditions

Citizen profile indicates eligibility for fee waiver (e.g., age over 70 for birth certificate, or disabled person with TELK ≥ 50% for address registration).

## Steps

1. Log in as a citizen over 70 years old
2. Submit an application for Certificate of Birth (standard fee: 5.00 BGN)
3. Verify system calculates the fee
4. Verify fee waiver is automatically applied
5. Verify fee is set to 0.00 BGN
6. Verify waiver reason is logged (e.g., 'Free for citizens over 70')
7. Verify citizen proceeds without payment step
8. Verify application status changes directly to IN_PROCESSING
9. Verify no receipt is generated (since no payment was made)

## Expected Result

System automatically detects citizen eligibility for fee waiver. Fee is set to 0.00 BGN. Waiver reason is logged. Citizen bypasses payment step entirely. Application proceeds directly to IN_PROCESSING status without requiring payment or generating a receipt.

## Test Data

Citizen: Age 72, Service: Certificate of Birth, Waiver Condition: Free for citizens over 70
