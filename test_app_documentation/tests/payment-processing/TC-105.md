# Multiple failed payment attempts trigger lockout

## Preconditions

Citizen has submitted a service application requiring payment. Citizen will attempt payment with invalid card details.

## Steps

1. Submit a service application with a fee (e.g., Certificate of Birth, 5.00 BGN)
2. Select 'Bank Card (Visa/Mastercard)' as payment method
3. Attempt payment with invalid card details (first attempt)
4. Verify payment fails and application remains in 'Awaiting Payment' status
5. Attempt payment with invalid card details (second attempt)
6. Verify payment fails again
7. Attempt payment with invalid card details (third attempt)
8. Verify payment fails for the third time
9. Verify application is locked for 1 hour
10. Verify citizen sees message: 'Multiple payment attempts detected. Please try again later or contact support.'
11. Attempt payment again immediately
12. Verify payment is blocked and lockout message is displayed
13. Wait 1 hour (or simulate time passage)
14. Verify citizen can attempt payment again after lockout period expires

## Expected Result

After 3 failed payment attempts, application is locked for 1 hour. Citizen sees message: 'Multiple payment attempts detected. Please try again later or contact support.' Payment attempts during lockout period are blocked. After 1 hour, citizen can retry payment.
