# Verify email notification sent when application is submitted

## Preconditions

Citizen has a registered account with verified email address. Email notifications are enabled in preferences.

## Steps

1. Log in as a citizen
2. Navigate to application submission page
3. Complete and submit an application for any government service
4. Check the email inbox for the registered email address
5. Verify the email notification is received

## Expected Result

Email notification is sent from no-reply@egovportal.bg with subject and body using the 'application_submitted' template. Email includes application reference number, service name, and unsubscribe link. Email is in HTML format with plain-text fallback.

## Test Data

Valid citizen account with email address
