# Verify rate limiting blocks non-critical notifications when limit exceeded

## Preconditions

Citizen account is registered. Multiple notification events can be triggered rapidly.

## Steps

1. Trigger 10 email notifications within one hour for the same citizen (e.g., multiple application status changes)
2. Verify all 10 emails are delivered
3. Trigger an 11th non-critical email notification within the same hour
4. Check email inbox
5. Verify the 11th email is NOT immediately delivered
6. Wait for the next available window (after 1 hour from first email)
7. Verify the queued email is now delivered

## Expected Result

Rate limiting enforces maximum 10 emails per hour per citizen. The 11th non-critical email is queued and delivered in the next available window. Critical notifications (password reset, account locked) would bypass rate limits.

## Test Data

Citizen account with multiple notification triggers
