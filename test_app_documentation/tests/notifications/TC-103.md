# Verify notification retry logic with exponential backoff for failed email

## Preconditions

Email service is configured to simulate failures. A notification event is ready to be triggered.

## Steps

1. Configure email service to fail on first 3 attempts
2. Trigger a notification event that sends an email (e.g., application submitted)
3. Monitor retry attempts and delays
4. Verify 1st retry occurs after 1 minute
5. Verify 2nd retry occurs after 5 minutes from 1st retry
6. Verify 3rd retry occurs after 30 minutes from 2nd retry
7. Allow email service to succeed on 4th attempt
8. Verify email is successfully delivered

## Expected Result

Failed email notifications are retried with exponential backoff: 1st retry after 1 minute, 2nd after 5 minutes, 3rd after 30 minutes, 4th after 2 hours, 5th after 12 hours. Email is marked as DELIVERED after successful retry. Retry delays match documented schedule.

## Test Data

Email service with simulated failures
