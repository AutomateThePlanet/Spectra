# Verify critical notifications bypass Do Not Disturb mode

## Preconditions

Citizen has enabled Do Not Disturb mode with scheduled hours (e.g., 22:00-08:00). Current time is within DND window.

## Steps

1. Log in as citizen and enable Do Not Disturb mode
2. Set DND schedule to suppress notifications during current time window
3. Trigger a non-critical notification (e.g., application status change)
4. Verify push and SMS notifications are suppressed
5. Trigger a critical notification (e.g., password reset or account locked)
6. Check all notification channels (email, SMS, push, in-app)
7. Verify critical notification is delivered on all applicable channels despite DND mode

## Expected Result

Non-critical notifications are suppressed during DND mode (no push/SMS). Critical notifications (password reset, account locked) bypass DND mode and are delivered on all applicable channels. Email and in-app notifications are delivered but without sound/vibration on mobile during DND.

## Test Data

Citizen account with DND enabled and scheduled
